Uber Re-Dispatch Case Study

Fixing the friction between algorithmic efficiency and rider trust.

← Back to Portfolio

1. The Problem

During high-stakes, time-sensitive trips (like traveling to the airport), Uber users experience severe anxiety and missed appointments when the algorithm silently re-dispatches their driver to someone further away.

Hypothesis: Uber's re-dispatch logic over-indexes on global marketplace efficiency at the cost of individual trip reliability.
